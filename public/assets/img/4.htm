Outfits (aka skins) are a type of cosmetic item players may equip and use for Fortnite: Battle Royle. Oultfits are cosmetic only, changing the appearance of the player's character, so do not provide any game benefit. Additionally, Outfits can come with Back Bling; the Back Bling can be worn independently of the outfit or not at all. Some outfits are also used as hero class skins in Fortnite: Save the World.

Outfits are generally obtained via Battle Pass seasons and through the Item Shop with V-Bucks. The following outfits are in the game:
